Your Essential Period Kit

Having a well-stocked period kit ensures you're always prepared and comfortable during your cycle. Here's everything you need to create your perfect period kit!

Essential Items

👜

A Bag for All Required Items

A cute, waterproof bag to keep all your period essentials organized and discreet.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Choose a waterproof material
  • • Pick a size that fits in your purse
  • • Consider a cute design you love
🩸

Pads And/Or Tampons

Stock up on your preferred sanitary products. Include different absorbency levels.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Pack 2-3 pads/tampons per day
  • • Include overnight protection
  • • Choose organic if possible
👙

Extra Underwear

Comfortable, breathable underwear for those unexpected moments.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Choose cotton for breathability
  • • Pack 1-2 extra pairs
  • • Dark colors are practical
💊

Pain Relief Medicines

Over-the-counter pain relievers for cramps and discomfort.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Ibuprofen or acetaminophen
  • • Check expiration dates
  • • Keep in original packaging
🧻

Tissues/Wet Wipes

For quick cleanups and maintaining freshness throughout the day.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Unscented wipes are best
  • • Pack travel-sized packs
  • • Include regular tissues too
🧴

Hand Sanitizer

For maintaining hygiene when soap and water aren't available.

💡 Pro Tips:

  • • Choose travel-sized bottles
  • • At least 60% alcohol content
  • • Unscented is preferred

Kit Maintenance Tips

🔄 Regular Updates

  • • Check expiration dates monthly
  • • Restock items as needed
  • • Clean your bag regularly
  • • Update based on your preferences

💡 Smart Tips

  • • Keep one at home and one in your bag
  • • Consider seasonal items (heating pad in winter)
  • • Pack according to your flow intensity
  • • Include emergency contact info
